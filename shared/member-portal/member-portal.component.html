Why Learn with Online Training?

Enhance your professional practice and training using scientifically based online Continuing Professional Development Programs (CPD) in collaboration with World Leading Academic & Healthcare Institutions. All programs are selected and approved exclusively by the Academic & Training Affairs at King Fahad Medical City.

  • Online convenience: Learn, track progress and measure knowledge retention from the convenience of your desktop, tablet or mobile device
  • Quality, accredited content: Access interactive, online programs developed by leading specialists and peer-reviewed by experts locally and around the world.
  • No cost to learn: Registration and Participation are free for King Fahad Medical City staff
